## The headline finding (shapes the whole campaign)

**No real-world program safely fuses all six pillars, and NO source covers
how to periodize six pillars without overtraining** — the research calls this
"the campaign's core design risk." Existing "Batman workouts" cover only
calisthenics + light strength + conditioning + token striking; gymnastics,
technical multi-discipline fight, and parkour are ADDITIVE and must be sourced
separately. → The campaign therefore actively MANAGES concurrent load: pillar
rotation, deloads, enhanced readiness gates, and volume caps on peak stages.

## Confirmed findings used

**Calisthenics structure** (SuperheroJacked, Darebee — blog, high conf.)
- SuperheroJacked Batman = 5-day strictly-bodyweight A/B + core + cardio,
  scalable up/down → the calisthenics spine.
- Darebee "Batcave Friendly" = no-equipment circuit (squats, punches,
  jumping lunges, push-ups, shoulder taps, slow climbers, sit-up punches),
  3/5/7 sets by level, capped at 10 → conditioning circuit shape.
- Martial arts + parkour are explicitly "sprinkle-in" separate pillars.

**Parkour beginner progression + safety** (Way of Ninja + multi-source
corroboration, high conf.) — landing (bend knees, absorb), safety/shoulder
roll FROM A SQUAT (head tucked, diagonal over one shoulder), precision jumps
to CLOSE targets (~1-2 ft) with a CLEARED landing, safety vaults, balance
work; sequence slowly then smooth. **Flips are advanced "beyond the basics",
deferred — never beginner content.** → baked into every parkour module +
campaign.safety_scaffolding.parkour_safety.

**Gymnastics wrist safety** (peer-reviewed — HIGHEST confidence)
- Wrist pain ~53% prevalence in adolescent gymnasts (systematic review), up
  to 75% chronic in male gymnasts (UCLA). Screen for front-of-wrist "pinch"
  pain (early warning → stop/regress); daily pre-practice lacrosse-ball
  soft-tissue prehab. → every gymnastics module opens with wrist prehab and
  a pinch-pain stop; advanced skills (levers, muscle-ups) are progression-
  gated.

## Not sourced → handled conservatively (open questions)

- **Six-pillar periodization**: no source. The campaign uses pillar rotation
  (one pillar leads heavy, others light), deloads after hard-camp blocks,
  enhanced readiness gates (joint soreness) on integration/peak stages, and
  volume caps — a conservative, safety-first fusion.
- **Per-discipline martial-arts drill vocabularies / "127 martial arts"**:
  not substantiated → "127 martial arts" is comic lore (fantasy-flagged);
  fight content uses boxing/Muay Thai/defensive counters (app disciplines).
- **Gymnastics skill progressions beyond wrist prehab** and **weighted-
  strength loading model**: not sourced → conservative well-known
  progressions, gated, with the sourced wrist safety wrapped around them.

## Design inferences

The 12-stage arc (calisthenics → strength → cardio → gymnastics I → parkour I
→ fight I → fight II → gymnastics II → conditioning peak → integration ×2 →
Become-the-Bat boss) and the pillar-rotation/deload safety model are the
campaign's own design, built on the verified pillar content + safety science.
Core rule «The body is a tool — sharpen it, don't shatter it.» encodes the
anti-overtraining discipline directly.
