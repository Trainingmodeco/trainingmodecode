Training Mode

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 20, 2026

Training Mode (“we,” “us,” the “app”) is a combat-fitness training web app available at apptrainingmode.com. This policy explains what information the app handles, why, and the choices you have. We keep this deliberately short because the app is built to keep your data on your own device wherever possible.

Information we handle

  • Account details (only if you sign in). Signing in is optional. If you choose “Continue with Google,” we receive your name, email address, and profile picture from Google so we can create and recognize your account. We do not receive your Google password.
  • Your training data stays on your device. Workouts, XP, levels, streaks, saved routines, custom combos, and app settings are stored locally in your browser (using localStorage) on the device you train with. This information is not uploaded to our servers.
  • Subscription status (only if you subscribe). If you purchase Pro, payment is processed by Stripe. We never see or store your card number. We keep a record that your account is entitled to Pro so the app can unlock those features.
  • Basic, privacy-friendly analytics. We may use aggregate, cookieless analytics to understand how many people use each feature. These figures are anonymous and are not used to identify or profile individual users.

How we use it

  • To create your account and keep you signed in across visits.
  • To save your progress and preferences so the app works the way you left it.
  • To unlock the features included in your subscription.
  • To understand, in aggregate, which features are used so we can improve the app.
We do not sell your personal information, and we do not show third-party advertising in the app.

Service providers

We rely on a small number of trusted providers to run the app:

  • Google — sign-in.
  • Supabase — stores your account record.
  • Stripe — processes subscription payments.
  • Netlify — hosts and delivers the app.

Each provider handles data under its own privacy policy and only to the extent needed to provide its service to us.

Your choices and rights

  • Use the app without an account. Most training features work without signing in.
  • Sign out at any time to end your session on that device.
  • Delete your local training data by clearing your browser’s site data for apptrainingmode.com.
  • Access or delete your account data. Email us and we will help you access or permanently delete the account information associated with your sign-in.

Children

Training Mode is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us and we will delete it.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes, we will update the date at the top of this page and, where appropriate, notify you in the app.

Contact

Questions or requests about your data? Email trainingmode.co@gmail.com.
