What Is Training Mode

Real Workouts, Built Like a Fighter's Game

Training Mode turns fitness and combat sports training into an anime arcade-style progression system. Show up, train with structure, and feel every session move you closer to the next level — on the mat and on screen.

Ways to Train

Every path earns XP. Every rep counts toward your next rank.

Workout builder, quick missions, cardio & conditioning circuits — structured strength for fighters.

🥊 Fight Mode

Combo coach, fight-focus rounds & technique practice across boxing, kickboxing, Muay Thai and MMA.

The bridge — strength + striking circuits that blend Fit & Fight into one engine.
