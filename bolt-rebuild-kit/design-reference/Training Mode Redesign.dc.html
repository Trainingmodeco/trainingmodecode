THE HOOK
PUNCH
A short, curved power punch thrown with a bent arm to the side of the target.
KEY POINTS
1Elbow bent ~90°, arm parallel to the floor.

COMMON MISTAKES
✕Swinging with a straight arm — kills power & balance.
